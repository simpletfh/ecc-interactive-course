How ECC saves time, reduces costs, and prevents mistakes with smart optimizations

You know how sometimes you have to repeat yourself to AI tools? "Remember we talked about this?" "What did I say last time?" ECC fixes that by remembering context across sessions.

Use fewer tokens, save money. ECC chooses the right model for each task and keeps prompts lean.

Remember across sessions. Your preferences, past decisions, and project context stick around automatically.

⚙️

Automation

Auto-run routine tasks. Tests, reviews, and pattern extraction happen while you focus on building.

Tokens = Money. Spend Wisely.

Every word you send to an AI costs money. ECC optimizes at every step to give you the same quality for 30-50% less.

1
Model Selection

Use cheaper models for simple tasks — haiku for edits, sonnet for planning.

2
System Prompt Slimming

Remove unused instructions and compress AGENTS.md to only what's relevant for the current task.

3
Background Processes

Offload long tasks to subagents. The main context stays small and responsive.

4
Iterative Retrieval

Load only relevant docs, not the whole codebase. Why pay to read files you don't need?

5
Parallel Execution

Run agents simultaneously instead of sequentially. Get results in half the time.
