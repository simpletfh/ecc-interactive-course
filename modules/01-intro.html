What is Everything Claude Code?

A performance coach that makes AI coding tools smarter, faster, and more reliable

The AI Coding Performance System

Imagine you open Claude Code and type /tdd to start a test-driven development workflow. Behind the scenes, a whole team of specialized AI agents springs into action to help you.

💡
Think of ECC as a Performance Coach

Like a coach who studies game film and designs plays, ECC doesn't write code for you — it makes your AI coding assistant smarter by orchestrating the right specialists at the right time.

What You'll Learn

⚡

What ECC Does

It's a plugin that coordinates 16 specialized AI agents, 65+ skills, and 40 commands to supercharge your coding workflow.

🎯

Why It Matters

Catch bugs earlier, write better code faster, and leverage AI more effectively — all while learning software engineering best practices along the way.

🔧

How It Works

Under the hood, ECC delegates tasks to the right specialist at the right time — just like a real software team with distinct roles and expertise.
